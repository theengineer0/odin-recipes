Saltimbocca alla romana

A recipe prepared with veal coated with raw ham and a sage leaf. It is a popular recipe in Rome and in the Lazio region

Ingredients for 1 person:

  • Calf meat
  • Sage
  • Raw ham
  • 50g of butter
  • Flour
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Flour the slices of veal
  2. Add a slice of ham and a sage leaf to the veal slices, securing them with a toothpick
  3. Heat the butter in a pan
  4. Cook the veal in butter adding pepper to taste
