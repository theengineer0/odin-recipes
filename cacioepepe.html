Cacio e pepe

An old recipe of central Italy, based on the classical ingredient that was most avaible in the area: pecorino cheese.

Ingredients for 1 person:

  • 100g of spaghetti
  • 50g of "pecorino romano"
  • A spoonful of peppercorns
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
  2. Grate the pecorino and put it on the plate
  3. Throw the pasta into the boiling water
  4. Crush the peppercorns with a pestle
  5. Put the pepper in a pan and heat it
  6. After a couple of minutes, add a little of the pasta cooking water to the pepper
  7. When the pasta is two minutes away from cooking, add it to the pan with the pepper and thus finish cooking
  8. Add the hot pasta to the plate with the cheese and mix
